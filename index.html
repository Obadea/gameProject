Pig Game

×

Number of Players: 2 or more (you can play alone, taking turns as different players)
Objective: Be the first player to reach a total score of 100 or more points.
Setup: 1. Each player starts with a total score of 0.
2. Decide who goes first (Player 1) through any mutually agreed-upon method.
Gameplay:
1. Player 1 begins the game by rolling a six-sided dice.
2. After the dice roll, the game generates a random number between 1 and 6 and displays it as the result.
3. If the result is 1:
a. Player 1's turn ends immediately.
b. All points accumulated by Player 1 during this turn are lost.
c. The total score remains unchanged.
d. The turn passes to the next player.
4. If the result is 2 to 6:
a. The result is added to Player 1's turn score.
b. Player 1 has the option to roll the dice again (go back to step 1) or hold their turn score (proceed to step 5).
5. If Player 1 decides to hold their turn score:
a. The turn score is added to Player 1's total score.
b. The total score is updated.
c. Player 1's turn ends.
d. The turn passes to the next player.
6. The next player (Player 2) now takes their turn, following the same steps 1 to 5.
7. Players take turns rolling the dice and accumulating points until one player's total score reaches or exceeds 100 points.
Winning:
The first player to reach a total score of 100 or more points wins the game. The game ends immediately, and that player is declared the winner.
Optional Rule:
If desired, players can agree on a higher or lower target score (e.g., 200 points) to make the game shorter or longer.
Note: The Pig Dice Game is a simple and fun game of chance and risk management. Enjoy and have fun playing!
